About the System

Serving the Municipality of Naic.

Built for our interns.

This attendance monitoring system was created for the On-the-Job Training program of the Municipality of Naic, Cavite. It replaces manual log-books with a paperless, secure, and instant workflow.

Whether you're an intern rendering your required hours or an administrator overseeing several batches, the system keeps every entry accurate and every report ready to submit.
